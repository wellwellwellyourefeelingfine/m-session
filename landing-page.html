What this is

A session guide for self-understanding

m-session provides structure and support for MDMA sessions focused on personal growth. Not therapy. Not treatment. A companion for honest self-reflection, whether you're on your own, with a partner, or with a trusted sitter. Built for people exploring relationships, self-awareness, and inner landscape through intentional experience.

Designed for altered states: minimal cognitive load, non-stimulating visuals, offline-capable, and always respectful of wherever you are in your process.

Session flow

Preparation through integration

Six phases guide you from intention setting to the days that follow. Each phase adapts to your choices and timing.

01
Preparation
Intake questionnaire, intention setting, harm reduction information, dosage guidance, and medication interaction checks. Build a personalized session timeline.
02
Come-up
Grounding meditations and body awareness exercises for the onset period. Breathwork, body scan, and gentle guidance to ease into the experience.
03
Peak
The heart of the session. IFS parts work, ACT values mapping, self-compassion meditation, coherence therapy, cognitive defusion, journaling, and open space.
04
Integration
As the experience evolves, reflective modules help you make sense of what surfaced. Letter writing, gratitude practices, and gentle re-grounding.
05
Closing
Honoring what happened. A message to your future self. One commitment to carry forward. A structured ending that creates a bridge to tomorrow.
06
Follow-up
Five time-locked modules that unlock over the following days. Check-ins, journal revisits, values compass follow-up, and integration reflection during the neuroplasticity window.
